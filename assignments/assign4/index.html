Leaflet Choropleth Map

The interactive map shows the proportion of Black K-12 students in each Iowa county, with pop-ups displaying county names and corresponding percentages. The legend is located on the bottom right of the map for reference. Additionally, a toggle button has been added to the top left of the map to indicate the location of Iowa State University.
